# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Hotel Booking
Complete hotel-only booking flow from search results through room and rate selection.

### 2. Flight Booking
Flight-only booking flow for outbound and return flight selection.

### 3. Packages
Combined flight + hotel package booking with flexible flight swapping capability.

### 4. Ancillaries
Additional travel products including tours, activities, car rental, and airport transfers.

### 5. Basket & Checkout
Unified shopping basket and complete checkout flow for all product types.
