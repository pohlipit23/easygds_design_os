# easyGDS

## Description
easyGDS is a comprehensive online travel booking platform that enables consumers to search, compare, and book flights, hotels, packages, tours, car rentals, and airport transfersâ€”all in one streamlined experience. The platform differentiates through exceptional user experience, making complex travel booking simple and intuitive.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Navigating Complex Travel Options
Booking travel requires navigating complex options across multiple products (flights, hotels, tours, transfers). easyGDS provides an intuitive, well-organized interface that makes comparing and selecting travel products effortless.

### Problem 2: Cluttered and Overwhelming Interfaces
Travel booking platforms are often cluttered and overwhelming, making it difficult to focus on what matters. easyGDS uses a clean, refined design with clear information hierarchy that prioritizes clarity and ease of use.

### Problem 3: Managing Multi-Product Bookings
Managing multi-product bookings (flights + hotels + tours) across different systems is cumbersome and time-consuming. easyGDS provides a unified shopping basket that lets travelers build and manage their entire trip in one place.

## Key Features
- Multi-product booking (flights, hotels, packages, tours, car rentals, airport transfers)
- Flexible package deals with flight swapping capability
- Shopping basket for building multi-component trips
- Dual hotel views (list and interactive map)
- Regional customization (currency, language, country)
- Fully responsive design (mobile and desktop optimized)
- Search refinement and filtering tools
- Real-time price comparison
- Integrated itinerary management
